launching   your   startup

Prototype landing page for startup product pre-release



With this design, I wanted to create a twist on standard sequential displays. I also intended for a minimalistic look with an easy navigation for users. The font and background colors are also standard, muted colors for a clean layout, and as to not draw attention away from the "keypoints" located on the hexagons.

I imagine this design to hold the company/organization name on the top left corner, and possibly a banner to replace the large white space on the home page. I also imagine the icons inside the hexagons to be replaced with company oriented text or images representing the key ideas of the product. A user's click will seamlessly send them to the respective key idea hexagon with a detailed description.
